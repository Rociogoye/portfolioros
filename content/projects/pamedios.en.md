## The project

PAMedios was my main work base for several years in Argentina and remotely. From there I managed communication, content, and campaigns for more than 20 clients with different needs, timelines, and goals.

This experience helped me understand businesses, organize priorities, coordinate profiles, and move work forward with editorial judgment and a strong focus on results.

## Context and problem

- Clients with different goals and messages that needed to be structured.
- Creative and technical teams that needed to work in sync.
- The need to turn broad briefs into concrete, publishable pieces.
- Projects with deadlines, last-minute changes, and multiple channels.

## My role

- Co-founder and communication consultant.
- Digital content and editorial calendar management.
- Coordination with designers, web profiles, and other collaborators.
- Review of visual, editorial, and brand consistency.
- Campaign metric analysis to suggest improvements.

## Working process

1. Brief intake and goal definition.
2. Turning business objectives into messages and deliverables.
3. Organizing content, timelines, and responsibilities.
4. Coordinating with design, web, photography, and video when needed.
5. Quality review before publishing or delivery.
6. Following results and making adjustments.

## Coordination and project management

- More than 20 clients with different needs.
- Work with designers, web profiles, and creatives.
- Ongoing communication with stakeholders to align priorities.
- Managing scope changes, deadlines, and delivery issues.
- Quality oversight to keep campaigns and pieces consistent.

## Strategic decisions and UX

- Prioritizing messages based on campaign goals.
- Reducing content when the channel needed immediacy.
- Adjusting tone and format to audience and funnel stage.
- Reviewing pieces with clarity, readability, and conversion in mind.

## Problems and solutions

- Problem: briefs were broad and underdefined.
- Solution: turn them into an action plan with priorities.
- Problem: too many interlocutors.
- Solution: organize flows and centralize reviews.
- Problem: inconsistent output across pieces.
- Solution: align message, format, and goal before production.

## Tools and platforms

- CMS and web publishing.
- Content management and editorial calendars.
- Design and presentation tools.
- Meta Ads and Google Ads for metrics and optimization.
- Coordination with creative and web teams.

## Result and impact

- More than 20 clients served.
- Campaigns, pieces, and content delivered with editorial consistency.
- Better alignment between communication, design, and business goals.
- Strong proof of seniority in coordination and strategy.
